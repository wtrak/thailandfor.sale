Property Buying Guide

1. Choose Your Area

Bophut (walkable, dining), Maenam (quiet & family-friendly), Lamai (relaxed value), Chaweng Noi (views, nightlife nearby).

2. Understand Ownership Options

Foreign buyers can own condos directly, while villas/homes usually involve leasehold or company structures.

3. Work with Trusted Partners

Always use vetted developers and independent legal counsel for secure transactions.

4. Plan for Income

Professional management can deliver strong rental returns when you’re not using the home.
